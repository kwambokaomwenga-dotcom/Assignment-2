# Assignment-2 
## About
Happy Tails is a pet-friendly platform dedicated to helping pets find loving homes while providing quality veterinary care and pet supplies.The platform connects pet owners with adoption services and veterinary care.

## Pages
Home– Landing page with services overview
About– Our story, mission, vision and team
Adopt a Pet – Browse available dogs, cats, rabbits and birds with filter by category
Appointment – Book a veterinary or adoption appointment
Vet Services– Veterinary care services including diagnostics, vaccinations, grooming and deworming
Success Stories– Stories of pets that found their forever homes
FAQs– Frequently asked questions on adoption, vet care and the pet shop
Contact– Contact form with validation and location details

## Features
Responsive design using Bootstrap grid system
Pet filter by category on the adoption page (JavaScript)
Contact form with live validation (JavaScript)
FAQ accordion (JavaScript)
Consistent warm beige colour scheme across all pages
