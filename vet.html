Diagnostic Services

We offer animal diagnostic services that diagnose illnesses or other problems in small, companion, and exotic animals as well as livestock and equine. Our animal clinic has professional veterinarians experienced in diagnostic testing.

Vaccinations

Preventive medicine is important to protect your pet against diseases that are incurable and fatal. We vaccinate against viral and bacterial diseases that include rabies, distemper, hepatitis, leptospirosis, parvovirus and parainfluenza in dogs and cat flu in cats.

Pet Grooming and Styling

Pet grooming services involve cleaning and caring for your pet's appearance and hygiene, including skin, nails, fur, ears, and teeth. Our animal clinic offers pet grooming services for dogs and cats, including bathing, nail trimming, brushing, haircuts, and ear cleaning.

Why Your Pets Need Grooming?

We offer the highest standards of professional pet grooming services. Here are obvious reasons why your pet should get professional grooming:

  • Access to the right grooming tools and products for your pet.
  • Experienced professional pet groomers.
  • Proper grooming for pets of all sizes and breeds.

Deworming

Animal deworming is the process of using certified dewormers to remove all types of internal parasitic worms, such as roundworms, tapeworms, hookworms, whipworms, and heartworms. The medications used for animal deworming vary depending on the parasite. This is why you need trustworthy veterinarians.

Our team is ready to assist whether you are dealing with cats, kittens, dogs, puppies, rabbits or birds. Call us today to book an appointment.
