Our Story

Happy Tails was founded by our director Stacy Omwenga. It was born as a solution to a common problem to pet-friendly households to ensure they are cared for and loved. It started as parents to Whiskers who made us ensure our animals can get us this fluffy, pure and unconditional love.

Like any other pet owners/parents we want something fun, exciting and safe for them but with good things does not mean great struggle and responsibilty. To prevent other pet owners from struggling we came up with Happy Tails to easen your work. We bring you handpicked, curated furballs top notch with great quality products and many more ideas.

As a parent you learn you cannot be perfect but there is perfection in imperfection we can try to be perfect but we ask you to be real parent in this journey of pethood.
